So, we recently, we participated in an exhibition of artisan's work and craftsmanship. It was an eye-opening experience for us. We were amazed by the skills and talents of these artisans. They create beautiful and unique pieces that are a testament to the rich cultural heritage of our nation. We showcased different types of horn made products, accessories, Jute weaving, embroidery, metalwork, furniture and many others. For us the exhibition was a great opportunity to bridge between the exporters and these artisans.

The notion of education through handicrafts rises from the contemplation of truth and love permeating life's activities.
--Mahatma Gandhi

It was heartening to see the visitors appreciating and buying our product also we got to support the artisans and their work Artisans are the backbone of any society. They are the ones who create the beautiful collection that represent the cultural heritage of a nation with aesthetics and quality. In recent times, artisans and their work should be getting the attention they deserve. It is vital that we protect our artisans and their creative work as it symbolises the importance of our nation. In conclusion, the exhibition was a fantastic experience for us. We showed them the beauty and talent of our artisans
