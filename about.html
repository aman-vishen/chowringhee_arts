About Chowringhee Arts

Chowrangee arts A brand inspired after a city that delineate its creativity, Heritage Aesthetics and historical importance .Our company takes pride in its excellent craftsmanship., quality and commitment to customer of our craft. Chowrangee arts is a Kolkata ( India ) based company engaged in manufacturing of leather goods, accessories and lifestyle products that are uniquely handcrafted and hand painted . We use the fusion of leather with other materials like Jute , Canvas, Silk, Cotton, Bamboo, etc. to give a modern trendy twist and an impeccable quality. Our manufacturing unit is based in Kolkata with a robust producing capacity of mentioned products and deliveries on time. We have our own sampling department creating new designs keeping in pace with upcoming global trends.

Women Empowerment

Women Empowerment By hiring more women and educating them professionally, we promote women's empowerment, creating sustained livelihood opportunities that result in a positive socio-economic impact. We believe in supporting societal goals and to support that, 1% of our proceedings goes towards a fund for our artisans. We also use 100% natural vegetable dyes for our products making them environmentally safe

Our Artisans/Craftmen

We aim to showcase our tradition our culture through this equisite range of leather products and horn made tableware with unique designs and patterns to global as well as indian markets . These little things truly add meaning and value to the monotony of everyday life.

MISSION AND VISION

To meet modern functional needs, we merge conventional, classic ideas with contemporary ones. Our company aims to shift a saturated market while retaining the traditional designs, but with a new, trendy twist to improve it. For our crafts, our people and our world, we believe in creating beautiful tomorrows. We are looking towards the future, practising and building sustainable, innovative and environmentally conscious processes.
